# Deal Desk Commercial Review

Use this skill when the task is packaging a commercial decision for approval or negotiation, not when giving generic legal theory. It is optimized for quote quality, pricing exceptions, renewal protections, and approval-ready decision notes.

## When to Use

- Reviewing a non-standard quote or pricing exception
- Preparing a deal approval memo before signature
- Checking renewal packages for risk and leverage
- Flagging commercial terms that require finance or legal review
- Structuring approve, approve-with-conditions, or escalate decisions
- Summarizing clause or pricing red flags for the deal team

## Workflow

1. Frame the commercial shape.
   State product scope, term, discount, renewal exposure, billing logic, and requested exception.
2. Separate gating checks.
   Distinguish pricing policy, legal review, renewal risk, and margin or cash-flow impact.
3. Document the tradeoff.
   Show what the business gains, what risk it accepts, and what condition reduces that risk.
4. Decide the escalation path.
   Name the approver, required edits, and fallback option.
5. Return an approval-ready packet.
   Keep the output usable by sales, finance, and legal without rewriting.

## Output Shape

- Deal summary: scope, term, pricing, exception
- Gating checks: finance, legal, procurement, renewal
- Risk notes: margin, cash timing, liability, lock-in
- Recommendation: approve, approve with conditions, escalate, reject
- Approval path: owner, approver, required edits, deadline

## Heuristics

- Non-standard discounts should always state the exchange value expected in return.
- Renewal terms matter as much as initial price.
- Approval notes should separate business upside from risk acceptance.
- If the fallback option is missing, the negotiation posture is weak.

## Anti-Patterns

- Approving price exceptions without renewal or payment review
- Sending a quote for signoff with no decision owner
- Mixing legal redlines and commercial asks into one vague note
- Treating every exception as urgent without ranking risk
